Pricing & Tax

Last updated: May 2026

1. Pricing

All prices on LegalKeys are displayed in GBP (£) and represent the full amount you will pay. There are no hidden fees.

2. VAT Status

LegalKeys is currently not VAT registered, as our annual turnover is below the UK VAT registration threshold of £90,000. No VAT is charged on any purchases made through this website. If our turnover exceeds the threshold in future, we will register for VAT and update our pricing and this policy accordingly.

3. Income Tax

As a sole trader, LegalKeys declares all income to HMRC and complies fully with UK income tax obligations under Self Assessment.

4. Currency

All transactions are processed in GBP. If your payment card is in a different currency, your bank or card provider may apply a conversion fee — this is outside our control.
