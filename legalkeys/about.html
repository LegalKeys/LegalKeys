About LegalKeys

A transparent, UK-based game key marketplace built on trust and legal compliance.

Who We Are

LegalKeys is a UK-based digital game key marketplace operated as a sole trader. We sell game activation keys for PC, PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo platforms, sourcing all stock through authorised distributors.

Our mission is simple: give gamers a safe, transparent place to buy keys — with none of the uncertainty that comes with unverified grey-market sites like G2A or Kinguin.

We work with verified distributor partners, ensuring all products are legitimately sourced.
