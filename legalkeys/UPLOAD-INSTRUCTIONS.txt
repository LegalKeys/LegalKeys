=====================================
LEGALKEYS — NAMECHEAP UPLOAD GUIDE
=====================================

STEP 1 — BUY HOSTING ON NAMECHEAP
-----------------------------------
- Go to namecheap.com → Hosting → Shared Hosting
- Choose "Stellar" plan (cheapest, fine for starting out)
- During checkout, select your existing domain: legalkeys.to
- Complete purchase

STEP 2 — ACCESS CPANEL
-----------------------
- Namecheap will email you cPanel login details
- Log in at: cpanel.legalkeys.to (or via Namecheap dashboard → Hosting → Go to cPanel)

STEP 3 — UPLOAD YOUR FILES
----------------------------
1. In cPanel, click "File Manager"
2. Navigate to the "public_html" folder (this is your website root)
3. Delete any default files in there (e.g. index.html placeholder)
4. Click "Upload" and upload ALL files from your legalkeys folder
   - Make sure hidden files are visible (.htaccess is a hidden file)
   - In File Manager: Settings → tick "Show Hidden Files"
5. Upload everything including the Legal/ folder and all PHP files

STEP 4 — RUN THE DATABASE SETUP
---------------------------------
1. In your browser, go to:
   https://legalkeys.to/setup.php?key=legalkeys-setup-2026
2. You should see a green "Setup Complete" page
3. IMMEDIATELY go to cPanel File Manager and DELETE setup.php
4. Log in at https://legalkeys.to/login.php with:
   Email: admin@legalkeys.to
   Password: admin123
5. Change the admin password straight away

STEP 5 — POINT YOUR DOMAIN TO NAMECHEAP HOSTING
--------------------------------------------------
(Do this in Namecheap → Domain List → Manage → Nameservers)
- Change nameservers to Namecheap BasicDNS (or the ones provided in your hosting welcome email)
- This will also let you add your Private Email MX records directly in Advanced DNS
- Allow up to 24 hours for DNS to propagate

STEP 6 — SET UP YOUR EMAIL
----------------------------
Once DNS is pointing to Namecheap hosting:
1. Go to Advanced DNS tab for legalkeys.to
2. Add these records for Private Email:
   - Type: MX | Host: @ | Value: mx1.privateemail.com | Priority: 10
   - Type: MX | Host: @ | Value: mx2.privateemail.com | Priority: 10
   - Type: TXT | Host: @ | Value: v=spf1 include:spf.privateemail.com ~all
3. Go to Private Email dashboard and create your mailbox
   (e.g. support@legalkeys.to)
4. Update the email in about.html to your real address, then re-upload that file

STEP 7 — ENABLE SSL (FREE)
----------------------------
1. In cPanel, find "SSL/TLS" or "AutoSSL"
2. Enable the free Let's Encrypt SSL certificate for legalkeys.to
3. Once active, uncomment the HTTPS redirect lines in .htaccess

STEP 8 — TEST EVERYTHING
--------------------------
□ Homepage loads correctly
□ Login works
□ Register works
□ Checkout page loads
□ Legal pages load (Terms, Privacy, Copyright, Pricing & Tax)
□ About/Contact page loads
□ Mobile menu works
□ Admin panel is password protected
